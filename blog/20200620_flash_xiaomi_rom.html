Flash xiaomi roms on Linux/MacOS

This is article explain briefly how to flash a xiaomi phone rom from a linux or mac computer without the need of xiaomi flash tool.

Note: Save all your data before processing.

Prepare the computer

On your computer install platform tools.

  • Go to platform tools download page
  • Download the SDK for your system
  • Unzip the archive
  • Open a terminal and go to unarchived directory
  • Execute pwd and keep the result

Then you need to download the fastboot rom for your phone.

  • Go to miui rom download page
  • Download rom for your model & variant
  • Untar the archive

Prepare the phone

First activate the developper options in the phone.

  • Open Settings
  • Scroll down to System then About phone
  • Tap Build number until you a see a message Developer Options enabled

Then enable ADB debugging.

  • Open Settings
  • Go to (System then) Developper options
  • Scroll down to debugging
  • Enable ADB Debugging

Reboot the phone in bootloader:

  • Conntect your phone to the computer
  • Unlock your phone
  • In the previous terminal execute:
./adb reboot bootloader
  • Accept the pairing on your phone
  • Your phone will reboot in bootloader mode

Flash the rom

  • Open a terminal an go to unarchived directory
  • Replace <pwd> with previous result and execute the following command:
sed -i 's,fastboot,<pwd>/fastboot,g' flash_all.sh
  • Execute the flashing script
./flash_all.sh

After a minute or two, the phone should reboot :)
